Smart Alarm • Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Smart Alarm is designed to help users wake up with challenge-based alarms in a simple, secure, and user-friendly experience. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Smart Alarm may collect limited information needed to provide core app features. Depending on how you use the app, this may include:

  • Account information, such as your email address when signing in with Firebase Authentication
  • Alarm settings, such as alarm time, label, challenge type, and preferences
  • Basic app usage data, such as alarm activity or simple statistics shown inside the app

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for app functionality, including:

  • Authenticating and managing your account
  • Saving and loading your alarm preferences
  • Supporting challenge-based alarm features
  • Improving app reliability and user experience

3. Data Storage

Smart Alarm uses a combination of secure services and local device storage:

  • Firebase Authentication is used for user login and account access
  • Firestore Database for user data may be used to save alarms, preferences, and statistics for app functionality

We aim to collect only the minimum amount of data necessary for the app to work properly.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is not shared with third parties except where required to operate the app, such as authentication services provided by Firebase.

5. User Control and Choices

You have control over your information. You may:

  • Log out of your account at any time
  • Delete app data by uninstalling the app or clearing local storage
  • Contact us if you have questions about your data

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information and keep the app secure. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app improvements, legal requirements, or service changes. Updates will be posted on this page.
